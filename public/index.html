Lawrence Thul

I’m an AI researcher with a passion for decision intelligence. I enjoy building systems that reason about uncertainty and make decisions autonomously. I have extensive experience in reinforcement learning, stochastic optimization, time series forecasting, and deep learning, in both academia and industry.

I earned my PhD from Princeton University’s CASTLE Lab under Professor Warren B. Powell, specializing in stochastic optimization and multi-agent sequential decision-making. I then served as Director of Artificial Intelligence at Optimal Dynamics for five years, leading the company’s AI and machine learning efforts from the early days through Series C.
